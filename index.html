You can use this Template from Google Spreadsheet to insert the data:

Once you have your information properly organized in columns [order] [characther] [group] you must get a RSS link. To do that, go to your Spreadsheet and select

  1. 1. File...
  2. 2. Publish to the web
  3. 3. Select the sheet
  4. 4. Click on "Start Publishing" (if not selected)
  5. 5. Click on [x] automatically republish
  6. 6. Get a link to the published data: RSS
  7. 7. Once you have the link, past it here (replace the one already pasted by default)
